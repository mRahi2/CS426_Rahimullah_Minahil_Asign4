OVERVIEW:
Assignmnets 2 featured a grab and snatch game where there are two teams:
- Red team: must build the pc build
- Blue team must take away/snatch the pc parts and prevent the red team from succeeding

There are 6 pc parts that will spawn randomly around the maze and must be picked up (using e) and droped (using e) at the base (orange box in the middle of map)
After all 6 parts are collected, the question's answer options will appear on screen.
